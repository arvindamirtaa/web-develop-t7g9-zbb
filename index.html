About the park

Welcome to the Park Of Canada, situated in the lap of the nature with lushful green trees surrounding it in the outskirts of the city. We offer many great rides for all age groups, come here to gorget all your worries and relieve your stress. A best place to have picnic and enjoy with your friends and family. Located to just north of Totonto, Canada's Park Drive offers world-class fun and thrill entertainment and features many attractions like roller coasters, Typhoons and Super Shot to have that Adrenaline rush and experience the thrill.

If you are looking to experience amazing things with your friends and family, don't miss out on the great rides all Spring, Summer and Fall, with many music concerts and performances occuring here it makes a perfect holiday destination. So come here to Canada Park Drive and forget all your worrieis and have fun all day long with our one-day pass, more information about tickets and passes on tickets page.

Address: Canada Park Drive, Vaughan, Ontario
